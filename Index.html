Sanctuary Guidelines

Our Sanctuary Promise

This is a sacred space of healing. Every voice deserves to be heard with respect and compassion.

Anonymity is Sacred

Your identity is protected here. Never ask for personal information. Share only what you're comfortable with.

Practice Compassion

Listen with empathy. Validate feelings. Offer support, not judgment. We're all healing together.

Protect the Sanctuary

No hate speech, threats, or harmful content. Report anything that violates our safe space.

Content Warnings

Use warnings for sensitive topics to help others prepare their hearts for your story.
